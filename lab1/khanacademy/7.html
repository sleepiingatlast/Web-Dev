Recipe #1: Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Sugar 1–2 tbsp
Baking powder 2 tsp
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Melted butter (or oil) 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.

Step 2: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter.

Step 3: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix gently.

Step 4: Heat a pan on medium and lightly oil it.

Step 5: Pour batter, cook until bubbles appear, flip, and cook 1–2 minutes more.

Recipe #2: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Garlic 4–6 cloves
Olive oil 4–5 tbsp
Chili flakes 1 tsp
Salt 1 tsp

Step 1: Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Save ½ cup pasta water.

Step 2: Slice garlic thinly. Warm olive oil in a pan on low–medium heat.

Step 3: Add garlic and chili flakes; cook until garlic is lightly golden (don’t burn).

Step 4: Add drained spaghetti and a splash of pasta water; toss until glossy.

Step 5: Add parsley, adjust salt, and serve (with parmesan if you want).

Recipe #3: Greek Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumbers 2
Tomatoes 2-3
Kalamata olives a handful
Feta cheese 150g
Olive oil 2-3 tbsp
Oregano 1 tsp

Step 1: Chop cucumbers and tomatoes into bite-size pieces.

Step 2: Slice onion thinly (soak in cold water 5 minutes if you want it milder).

Step 3: Add olives and pepper (optional).

Step 4: Top with feta.

Step 5: Mix olive oil + lemon/vinegar + oregano + salt/pepper, then drizzle and toss gently.
